About

My current practice explores the idea of photography being used as a medium for art therapy. I am currently exploring the effect my knee has had on me since my surgery.

Using a medical approach to my work is enabling me to show how the personal and medical can connect. Using my own medical records, a model of a knee and various other aspects of my recovery I am creating a body of work which enables the viewer to see how all of this connects.

I am still incorporating my own personal aspect of my recovery into the work, but by also using medical references it makes the work slightly more universal. By doing this it allows more than just me to connect to the piece.

By looking into how the knee works and different medical conditions the knee can suffer has taught me about the joint. Also researching into artist such as Jo Spence has helped me gain more of an understanding as to how other artist have approached the medical and the personal.
